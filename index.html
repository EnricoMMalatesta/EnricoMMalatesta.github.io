I am an Assistant Professor at the Computing Sciences Department at Bocconi University in Milan.

My research lies at the interface of theoretical physics and computer science. I am particularly interested in the statistical mechanics of disordered systems and their interdisciplinary applications, including constrained optimization problems, machine learning, and high-dimensional statistics.

For more information see my publications and CV.
